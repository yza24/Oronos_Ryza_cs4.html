Bicol Region Hotspots

The Bicol Region comprises the southern part of Luzon, the largest island in
the Philippine archipelago. The total land area is 5.9% of the total land area
of the country. Around 69.3% of the total land area is alienable and disposable
while the remaining 30.7% is public forest areas.
